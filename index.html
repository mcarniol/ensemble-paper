How the picks are made

V18 is a multi-factor long-only quant strategy. Each month it ranks roughly 3,000 U.S.-listed names on a composite of value, profitability, low risk, dividend, share-issuance discipline, and a forward-looking auditor red-flag avoidance signal. The top decile (~30 names) is held, with sequential-replacement at exit-rank 3× the target portfolio size.

Two overlay sleeves modulate the book during regime shifts: a T6.5 anchor-GLD position when correlation regimes flip risk-off, and a time-series-momentum slice across SPY/TLT/IEF/TIP/GLD/DBC.
